Beagle

10-11kg

Short haired, hard coat of medium lengh

Many colors

The beagle is a breed of small hound that is similar in apperance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle is a scent hound, developed primarily for hunting hare (beagling). With a great sense of smell and superior tracking instinct , the beagle is employed as detection dog for prohibited agricultural imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent but single-minded. It is a popular pet due to its size , good temper , and lack of inherited health problems.
